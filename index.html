Our Story

The idea for Doctorblast began in the year 2020 while the current CEO, Dr. Elly Inda, was in his second year of his medical school training during which he had volunteered as a teacher in Kiboro Primary School in Mathare, Kenya with PACEMaker International — a non-profit organization that serves to provide quality education in resource-limited areas. Seeing how the kids there had no immediate access to emergency health care response yet having medical conditions ranging from Epilepsy to Heart defect which require prompt medical attention, his heart was deeply moved by the plight of the children from these areas. It was from there that he began to train the pupils on basic life saving procedures. In the steps of The Great Physician, he however felt that this was not enough as there was still a great need to spread The Gospel of Christ therein, thus launching Discipleship and Bible study classes on the same. He believes that one student touched is a whole family touched and with that the whole community is touched.

Dr. Elly Inda
